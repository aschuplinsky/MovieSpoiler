Movie Quote

Jacob: I don't know if I picked that circus. But something told me that circus picked me.

Plot

Water for Elephants is a movie about a young man named Jacob that ran away after finding out that his parents died. He found himself on a circus train. After falling in love with the boss's wife, Marlena, he has to fight to save himself, his love, and the circus animals he had grown to care for. In the end, the boss, August, is killed when the elephant, Rosie, is protecting Jacob. Because August is gone, Jacob and Marlena were able to be together. They took Rosie, had a family, and were happy for the rest of their lives.
